a comic about loss.
made in Maya, fall 2021

For this piece, I utilized Maya to create the environment for this scene shown in the comic. In each panel the environment, tree, and leaf are lit differently with volumetric fog, ambient and directional lighting.

After being rendered in Arnold, they were assembled into this comic format. The piece evokes the fear and anxiousness as a result of climate change and the wildfires that plague the Western US.
